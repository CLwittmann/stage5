About Stage5

And because our in-house design team consists of many seasoned engineers, we have the expertise and know-how to produce the most superior and advanced modular, lightweight, custom designs that perfectly fit our clients' needs every time. Stage 5 Staging's equipment is manufactured at our facility by our vast and dedicated fabrication team, which boasts a combined total of more than 200 years of hands-on manufacturing experience. When it comes to labor, not only are we proud of our hard-working staff in our manufacturing plant, but our patented, proprietary labor-saving techniques have established Stage 5 Staging as the industry leader in efficiency. Be assured that everyone at Stage 5 Staging, from our administrative, sales, design, project management, and fabrication staff, is committed to ensuring that our equipment always meets and exceeds all customer expectations.

Contact Us

Stage 5 Staging takes great pride in offering the finest in modular, lightweight, portable performance stage platforms, seating risers and accessories to customers worldwide. In addition to our portable staging products, which are all designed and manufactured in-house, we also have a custom ornamental rail division, Railing Concepts, that engineers, manufactures and installs rails to your design concepts. We have provided superior product solutions for stadiums, arenas, ballparks, convention centers, theaters and production companies, as well as educational, worship and governmental organizations, since 1990.Our company and staff designers continuously consult with designers and architects around the world, ensuring that we are always at the forefront of technology and that our products are second to none.
